Bugzilla 3.6

Bugzilla 3.6 is the next major release after Bugzilla 3.4. The major focus of Bugzilla 3.6 was cleaning up any rough edges of existing features in Bugzilla, and fixing various usability issues discovered during a research study on Bugzilla at Carnegie-Mellon University. Bugzilla 3.6 also has the new Extensions system and the new migration system.

Schedule:

  • Feature Freeze: December 8, 2009
  • First Release Candidate: March 08, 2010
  • Final Release: April 13, 2010
