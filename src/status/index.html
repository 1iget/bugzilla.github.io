Status

If you'd like to keep up with what's going on with the Bugzilla Project, you can follow the Bugzilla Update Blog. Updates are published fairly regularly (about every two weeks) and almost every time that a release comes out.

If you administer a Bugzilla, you are advised to also subscribe to the very low-volume Bugzilla Announce mailing list.

If you'd like to see what's changed in Bugzilla over time, or see detailed change lists between releases, check out our Change Log page.
